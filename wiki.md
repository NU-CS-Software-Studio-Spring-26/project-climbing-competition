# Distributed Climbing Comp Wiki

## Background

Climbing competitions are traditionally held in person at local gyms or outdoor gatherings. However, the introduction of climing boards (ie. Kilter Board, Tension Board, Moon Board) allows for the possibility of asynchronous, virtual bouldering competitions.

Our project focuses on the Kilter Board (but can be expanded to include any board). The Kilter Board is an app-controlled climbing training wall with adjustable angles and LED-lit holds.
